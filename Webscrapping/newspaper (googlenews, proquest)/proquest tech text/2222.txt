AUSTRALIAN LABOR PARTY PORT Augusta-based candidate Julie Watson grew up in Port Lincoln and was raised by a single mum, who was a youth worker.
She taught Ms Watson that all people deserve fairness and justice in their lives.
Ms Watson wants to see the Upper Spencer Gulf lead the way in building Australia's clean energy future. She wants to see Grey communities receive the long-term benefits of the renewable energy boom.
