FROM an army of drones and robot tractors, to virtual reality farm simulations, the inaugural AdvanceAg agtech showcase will let farmers see the next evolution of on-farm technology. To be held at the Adelaide Showground on February 24, the event will be headlined by Israeli start-up funder and Terra Venture Partners' founder Astorre Modena.
