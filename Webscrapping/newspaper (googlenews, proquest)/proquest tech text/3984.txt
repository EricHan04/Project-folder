THE national finals of a school robotics competition will be held in Adelaide, in December, after receiving $15,000 of State Government funding.
The Vex Robotics Competition will involve student teams in a game-based engineering challenge to design and build competing robots.
The event is expected to attract about 750 people to the Adelaide Convention Centre, including 600 from interstate.The top 12 teams will go on to the international tournament in Louisville, Kentucky in April 2020.
