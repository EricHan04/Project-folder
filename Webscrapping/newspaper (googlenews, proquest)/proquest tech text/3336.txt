Sheep and wool were a major focus of the 110th Mundulla Show, with this year's theme Click Go the Shears.
About 2500 people came through the gates - double last year, when hot weather impacted attendance.
The prime lamb competition, which attracted about 30 entries, was still a spectacle, despite numbers being back from the record 54 entries last year.
In the Innovation Expo, 16 exhibitors showcased the latest agricultural technology.
This included Think Digital, which had a virtual reality set up showing young people where their food comes from.
The Rothe family, Tolhverrae stud, Padthaway, claimed the championship double in the beef cattle section with a Simmental bull and heifer.
