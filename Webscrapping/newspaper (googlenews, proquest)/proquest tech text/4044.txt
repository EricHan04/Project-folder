Two Yirara College students, Telisha Watson and Jacob Yunupingu, have been successful in their applications to further their education.
They are with Santos Karnkanthi Indigenous Engineering School in partnership with Wirltu Yarla Aboriginal Education at the Adelaide University, in Adelaide, During Engineering School in July, the students had the chance to take part in the Adelaide Oval Roof Climb, tour at the Adelaide Oval, visit Santos Head Office, Santos training Centre and Core Library.
Telisha and Jacob, along with other successful Indigenous students will participate in many fun activities including coding activities, playing with robots, expanding their understanding of engineering and how it fits into our everyday world.
Yirara students have many opportunities to experience engineering skills, with classes in robotics, mechanics, and digital technology.These skills are keeping our young indigenous people current with the technological world.
