For many of us it's hard to escape the noise from our roads, trains and planes and the World Health Organisation has guidelines about the recommended levels we should be exposed to, because of the links to adverse health effects. But the same can't be said for wind turbines and that's because there's not enough evidence to determine if they're needed. A team of researchers at Flinders University in South Australia is nearing the end of a study, which may help change that.
