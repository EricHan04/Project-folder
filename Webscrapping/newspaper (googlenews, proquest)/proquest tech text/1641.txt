VIRTUAL reality could be among a suite of solutions to help tackle the labour shortage in the horticulture sector - if a plan to build digital ideas to help solve on-farm problems comes to fruition.
It was one idea pitched in response to a meeting of AgTech startup businesses meeting with growers to help solve common industry problems. A group of agricultural start-ups headed to the Riverland to hear first-hand from growers.
ThincLab's Eloise Leaver said the trip stirred up conversations about the problems faced by farmers.
