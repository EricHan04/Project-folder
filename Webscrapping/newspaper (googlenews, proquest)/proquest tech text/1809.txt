AN event to advance farming technology will be held again next year at the Adelaide Convention Centre.
More than 300 people had attended Advance Ag last year.
“The first Advance Ag held earlier this year was a resounding success with 87 per cent of attendees stating they would likely attend the event again,” said Primary Industries and Regional Development Minister David Basham Elders chief executive Mark Allison said: “Elders recognises, more than ever, the importance of innovation and the advancement of AgTech to match the needs of producers across Australia.”
