YESTERDAY morning His Excellency the Governor, as program patron, addressed the schools participating in the student expo of the Concept2Creation Program of Northern Advanced Manufacturing Industry Group Inc at the Golden Grove Arts Centre.
In the afternoon the Governor, as patron-in-chief of the UniSA - Bob Hawke Prime Ministerial Centre, and Mrs Scarce hosted a lunch with the Annual Hawke Lecture.
In the evening at the Adelaide Town Hall, the Governor and Mrs Scarce attended the 12th Annual Hawke Lecture followed by refreshments. In the morning, Year 6 students from St Peters Girls School toured Government House.
