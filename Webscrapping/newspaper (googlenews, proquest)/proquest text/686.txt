ELLEX Medical Lasers has signed a contract to assemble devices for fellow Adelaide technology company Signostics.
Ellex will carry out the sub-assembly of Signostics' hand-held ultrasound device, the Signos RT.
Ellex chief executive Tom Spurling said 800 units would be targeted to be built in the next six months and the agreement was expected to continue.
He said it was part of Ellex's strategy to identify new revenue streams.
"This is an important opportunity to ensure the optimal utilisation of our highly skilled manufacturing team and to diversify our earnings," Mr Spurling said.
