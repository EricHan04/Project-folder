AN APP that allows people to monitor the welfare of their elderly relatives has won a national award.
CareApp, based in Tonsley, was the joint overall winner at the National Information Technology in Aged Care Awards, announced in Adelaide.
It followed creator Allison Nikula taking out the emerging innovator category at SA’s Winnovation Awards in October. CareApp provides an insight into how people’s loved ones are faring through reliable and reassuring updates.It aims to bridge the knowledge gap between a family member, their loved ones, carers and aged care providers.
