YESTERDAY morning at Government House His Excellency the Governor received Lyn Breuer MP, Speaker of the House of Assembly.
Afterwards the Governor, as a guest of the Adelaide Hills Council, launched a report at the Unley Civic Centre.
Later the Governor, as patron of the Northern Advanced Manufacturing Industry Group, addressed the gathering at the Concept2Creation Expo at Golden Grove Arts Centre.
In the evening at Government House the Governor, as Chief Scout, and Mrs Scarce hosted a reception to thank supporters and volunteers of Scouts Australia - SA Branch.
