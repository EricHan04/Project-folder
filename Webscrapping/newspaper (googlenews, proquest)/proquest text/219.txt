YESTERDAY morning, His Excellency the Governor, as patron of Foundation Daw Park, attended the 4th Remembrance Day Business Breakfast at the InterContinental Adelaide.
Afterwards, the Governor, as patron, addressed the guests and presented plaques to schools participating in the Student Expo of the Concept2Creation Program of NAMIG (Northern Advanced Manufacturing Industry Group) at Golden Grove Arts Centre.
In the afternoon, Mrs Scarce participated in the judging for the Playford Alive Achievement Awards at the Riverside Centre.
In the evening, the Governor, as patron-in-chief of the Australia Day Council of South Australia, accompanied by Mrs Scarce, presented an award to the South Australian finalist in the Australian of the Year Award at the SA Finalists function hosted in conjunction with the National Australia Day Council at the National
Wine Centre.
