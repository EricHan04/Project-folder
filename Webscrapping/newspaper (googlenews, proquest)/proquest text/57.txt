ADELAIDE Angels smashed 16 runs on the way to an impressive 16-3 victory over Henley & Grange in round two of SA Baseball.
The Angels are the early season big improvers with two wins from two and sit second on the ladder.
Leading the way is Sturt which registered a 5-0 win over East Torrens, while Golden Grove Central Districts and Glenelg - which scored four runs in the final inning to beat Goodwood Indians 5-1 - also won their second games.
Reigning premier Kensington Cardinals led 4-1 against West Torrens after seven innings but lost 4-5 when the Eagles made a late charge with a pair of runs in each of the final two frames.
Port Adelaide broke the game open late to beat Northern Districts 10-2.
