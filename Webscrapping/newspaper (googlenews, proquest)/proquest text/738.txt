START-UP conference Southstart will return in February for a four-day event held in venues across Adelaide and in regional areas.
Tickets to the event - known as the “Emergence” fesival - will go on sale on Wednesday. It will include a series of keynotes, workshops, masterclasses, panels and summits covering a diverse range of sectors. An investor summit will bring together more than 30 representatives from venture capital firms, while a national start-up showcase will result in 20 start-ups pitching to investors. The Emergence festival will be held from February 16 to 19, with tickets available on the Southstart website.
