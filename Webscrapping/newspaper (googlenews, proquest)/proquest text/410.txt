Meningie Area School is one 50 taking part in the nation's first large scale roll-out of 3D printing technology across the state.
The roll-out is a joint initiative between the State Government, public schools and Adelaide-based educational technology company Makers Empire, and is designed to engage students in STEM using 3D technology.
State Education Minister Susan Close said it is important young people learn about new and emerging technologies from an early age.
