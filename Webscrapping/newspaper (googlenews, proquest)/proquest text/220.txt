YESTERDAY morning at Government House, His Excellency the Governor received the Honourable Lyn Breuer MP, Speaker of the House of Assembly.
Later the Governor addressed the Concept2Creation Expo.
In the afternoon, the Governor and Mrs Scarce hosted Mr R Champion de Crespigny AC and Mrs M Champion de Crespigny and Mr A Fletcher, Chief Executive Officer of Defence SA, and Mrs J Fletcher.
In the evening the Governor, as patron of SA Defence Industry Leadership Program of the Defence Teaming Centre, presented awards at the Program's 2011 Graduation Ceremony.
Afterwards at the National Wine Centre, the Governor addressed the Submarine Technology Conference Dinner.
