AN industry-led skills program in northern Adelaide will run at least until the end of next year, despite withdrawal of Federal Government funding in December.
The Northern Advanced Manufacturing Industry Group (NAMIG), which runs the Concept2Creation program for students, in collaboration with local schools, will receive $700,000 in funding from the Department of Trade and Economic Development and the Department of Further Education, Employment, Science and Technology.
The C2C program has run since 2005.
