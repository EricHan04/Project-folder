BASEBALL: Adelaide Angels have staked a claim as a team on the rise and a real finals prospect after winning a double-header against East Torrens on Sunday. Goodwood remained clear on top of the table with 11-1 and 13-2 victories over Northern Districts. Port Adelaide and Woodville both moved up the ladder with two-game sweeps of West Torrens and Golden Grove Central Districts respectively, while Glenelg moved into second spot by beating Sturt twice.
