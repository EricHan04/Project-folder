YESTERDAY morning, His Excellency the Governor addressed the gathering and presented awards at the "Concept2Creation" student expo at the Golden Grove Arts Centre, convened by the Northern Advanced Manufacturing Industry Group. In the afternoon at Government House, the Governor, as patron of the Service to Youth Council, and Mrs Scarce hosted a reception to acknowledge the achievements of young people associated with the council.
In the evening, the Governor, as patron-in-chief of the Australia Day Council of South Australia, presented the South Australian of the Year 2009 Award at the SA finalists function at the National Wine Centre. Afterwards, the Governor, as a guest of the Adelaide United Football Club, attended the Asian Champions League Final - second Leg at Hindmarsh Stadium.
