THE future of SA start-ups-focused SouthStart Conference has been secured by a new group of organisers, The Moonshine Lab. SouthStart event director Craig Swann, pictured fourth from left above, said the event was “a stage and a platform to share and celebrate the incredible innovation that is happening in SA”. The core team will be doing all it can to make a statement and remain sustainable as it looks to boost funding and attendances at the annual event, which will be held in November this year.PAGE 27
