MOUNT Lofty visitors can access a new free app to help identify major landmarks on the Adelaide skyline.
The State Government, with the help of a local tech company Lateral Vision, has developed the Lofty 360 augmented reality app to be used by visitors when taking in the views from the 727m-high summit.The app can be used on mobile devices to identify locations such as Kangaroo Island and the Barossa Valley from the summit, and provide more information about those locations.
