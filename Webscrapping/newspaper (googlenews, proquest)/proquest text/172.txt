ADELAIDE-based accounting firm Harmer and Associates has joined fast-growing national group William Buck.
Under the merger, Harmer and Associates director Chris Gebhardt will become a director of William Buck South Australia. Mr Gebhardt said the deal gave clients and staff access to a broader range of resources and opportunities.
"By joining William Buck, we are able to build on our traditional business foundations of taxation and business services and expand our service offerings into assurance and consulting," he said.
William Buck - which has offices in Adelaide, Sydney, Melbourne, Perth, Brisbane, Cairns and Auckland - increased its revenue by 34.1 per cent to $59 million in 2002-03.
