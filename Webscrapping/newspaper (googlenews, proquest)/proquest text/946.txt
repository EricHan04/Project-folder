In November last year, an Adelaide tech company, Voxon Photonics, drew considerable attention to the SA industry via a $1 million investment for its Voxiebox, which creates moving holograms, or 3D digital content, without the need for users to wear special glasses. Founded by Will Tamblyn and Gavin Smith, potential applications for the technology include gaming, education, engineering, medical imaging and advertising.
