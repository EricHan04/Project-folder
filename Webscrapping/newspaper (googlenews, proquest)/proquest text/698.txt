STRONG growth in US sales helped Adelaide medical devices manufacturer Ellex to gain a 27 per cent lift in revenue to $54.3 million.
Operating profit before tax to the end of June rose to $1.3 million. The company said: "The improved operating performance is a direct result of significant sales growth, primarily in the USA where revenues were up 124 per cent." Ellex Medical Systems makes laser systems for diagnosis and treatment of eye disease.
It also contract manufactures parts for SA firm Signostics.Sales in Japan declined, while growth in the Europe and Middle East markets was below expectation. However, Asia results were strong, driven primarily by sales into China.
