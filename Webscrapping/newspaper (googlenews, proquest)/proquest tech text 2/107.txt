THE runaway success of innovative businesses at Adelaide’s Lot Fourteen has prompted Chief Entrepreneur Andrew Nunn to declare SA on the cusp of becoming an economic powerhouse.
Start-up companies at Lot Fourteen have raised more than $90m in capital in just three years and will soon employ more than 600 people.
Mr Nunn said SA was just “three good decisions away” from an economic boom. REPORTS, PAGES 6-7
