AUSTRALIAN software firm Atlassian has agreed to acquire six-year-old start-up AgileCraft, which helps businesses plan collaboratively among teams, for $US166 million.
The US-listed company announced the deal after another record high closing price.
AgileCraft will cost Atlassian $US154 million in a cash payment, with the rest paid out in shares.
Atlassian said the deal would add $US1 million to $US2 million to revenue in the 2019 financial year, which should be three-quarters complete by the time the acquisition closes next month.The company’s shares closed have gained 87.7 per cent in the past year, pushing the software company’s market capitalisation higher than $US25 billion.
