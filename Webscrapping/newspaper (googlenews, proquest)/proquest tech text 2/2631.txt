IT’S official and truthfully, we knew it, Adelaide is the most honest city in the country, and third in the world according to a new study.
Despite highbrow east coast types forever claiming superiority, it seems our “big country town feel” counts for something, namely sincerity.
German data analytics group Twinner released a ranked list of cities this month, compiling data on the reputation of car dealerships, government transparency, theft and more to determine honesty.
In 75 prominent cities around the world, Adelaide came in as the third most honest, behind Zurich and Tokyo who took out first and second respectively. It comes after Adelaide was named the third most liveable city in the world in June.
