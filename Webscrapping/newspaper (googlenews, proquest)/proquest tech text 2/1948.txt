HELPING food, wine and agribusiness operators improve profits and grow their workforce is the driver behind the South Australian Regional Development Conference in Tailem Bend this month.
The May 23-24 conference at The Bend will explore topics including what agribusiness can learn from luxury brands, innovations in packaging, the next wave of agriculture technologies and artificial intelligence in the food industry.It will give access to internationally-recognised food, wine and agribusiness experts.
