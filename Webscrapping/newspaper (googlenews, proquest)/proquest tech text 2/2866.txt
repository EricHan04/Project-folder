INDEPENDENT candidate for South Australia’s most marginal seat Jo Dyer faces accusations of hypocrisy for taking anonymous donations through a crowd-funding website.
Ms Dyer, a critic of Christian Porter’s use of a blind trust funded by unnamed donors to fight a defamation case against the ABC, has attracted 80 anonymous donations.
The outgoing Adelaide Writers’ Week director said she knew the identities and would publish them. FULL REPORT PAGE 6
