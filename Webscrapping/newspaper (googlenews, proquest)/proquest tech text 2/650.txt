FORMER Adelaide Crows ruckman Brad Moran is celebrating a $205m payday following the sale of his start-up software company.
The sale of CitrusAd to French multinational advertising firm Publicis Groupe comes a decade after Mr Moran’s AFL career at the Crows was cut short by injury.
Mr Moran, 35, said his retirement from football was a “sliding doors” moment that kickstarted his business, which enables supermarkets to charge brands a premium for the best product placements on their online stores. FULL REPORT PAGE 9
