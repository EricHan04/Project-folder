THE Willunga Farmers Market has announced the winner of its fifth Farmers Scholarship.
Chris and Bridgett Day, of Yankalilla, run a “regenerative” farming system. The couple aim to regenerate the land through farming practices and management. They want to increase their lamb production, expand into long-storage garlic varieties, medicinal, culinary and indigenous herbs, and fruit suited to the Fleurieu Peninsula.
They believe a diversity of crops is important in an increasingly unpredictable climate.The Days have won a scholarship of $12,000 for start-up funds and $3000 in mentorship and business training.
