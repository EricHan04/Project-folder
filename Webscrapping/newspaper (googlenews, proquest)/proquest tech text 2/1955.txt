JOBSEEKERS will need to learn about artificial intelligence and how it can contribute to their organisation, role and industry to stand out from the crowd in a 2019 job search.
Recruiters Hays reports jobseekers should look for opportunities to upskill to gain basic AI competency and understand how it can be used to improve their productivity.
They should mention this knowledge in their resume to make it clear to employers that they are ready to perform their job alongside AI.
Managing director Nick Deligiannis says it is important for jobseekers to stay on top of the latest trends if they want their career to advance.“Given the pace of change, the only thing certain in 2019 is that a jobseeker’s capacity to learn will start to become more valuable than the hard skills they already possess,” he says.
