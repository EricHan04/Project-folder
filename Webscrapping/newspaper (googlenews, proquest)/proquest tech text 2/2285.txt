THE pandemic has exposed security flaws in smart-home technology that’s giving skilled hackers an “open door” into Aussie properties.
Researchers have unearthed technical glitches in hundreds of millions of devices in recent weeks, leaving homes and firms vulnerable to attacks.
And security professionals say homeowners are failing to take basic precautions when installing smart devices, such as simply not changing default password settings.
It comes as home workers and businesses are having their vital files hijacked and held to ransom by international cyber criminals. SPECIAL REPORTS, PAGES 10-11
