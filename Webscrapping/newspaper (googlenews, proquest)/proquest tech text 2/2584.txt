ADELAIDE – home of the $2.3 billion RAH – has been rated the world’s 28th best “hospital city”, beaten by Melbourne, Sydney, Brisbane and Perth.The list was compiled by British digital health provider Medbelle, which rated Tokyo as the world’s top hospital city, followed by Boston, London and Paris. Factors were split into three categories – infrastructure, quality of care, and access. Medbelle managing director Daniel Kolb said the project wanted to look at the “bigger picture” of hospital care. Melbourne was ranked seventh and Sydney 11th.
