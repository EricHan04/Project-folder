A NATIONAL program to track the spread of – and devise treatments for – antibiotic resistant superbugs is among 10 bold research projects boosted by grants from a pool of $10 million.
Federal Health Minister Greg Hunt announced the latest recipients from Medical Research Future Fund’s Frontier program, which gives researchers up to $1 million to develop a plan for their innovative idea that could significantly improve human health.
University of Sydney researchers will use sensors, data and artificial intelligence to study how resistant bacteria spread and how to treat them.Other projects include developing a way to assess lung function without X-ray radiation.
