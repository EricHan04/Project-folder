AN innovative Catholic girls’ college is the only South Australian school in the running for a national education award.
Loreto College at Kensington Park has built new science and library facilities while changing its curriculum to include contemporary subjects such as artificial intelligence, netball, global cuisine, fashion design, photography and AFLW. New programs have been introduced to provide improved social, emotional and academic support to students and monitor their wellbeing.
Loreto is one of five schools nominated at the 2022 Australian Education Awards for having the best strategic plan.
It is the second year in a row that Loreto has been a finalist at the awards, regarded as the country’s most prestigious for independent education. The winners will be announced on Friday.
